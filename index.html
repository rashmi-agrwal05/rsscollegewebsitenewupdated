About the College

Dr. Raj Shekhar Shrivastava Para Medical College is committed to providing high-quality education in paramedical sciences, nurturing competent healthcare professionals to serve society with dedication and skill.

Our Mission

To impart comprehensive paramedical education with practical exposure.

Our Vision

To be a leading paramedical institute known for excellence and integrity.

Courses Offered

Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technician (DMLT)

Hands-on laboratory diagnostics and clinical testing training.

Diploma in Operation Theatre Assistant (DOTA)

Professional training for operation theatre procedures and management.

Diploma in Ophthalmic Assistant (DOA)

Specialized education in eye care and ophthalmic practices.

Certificate in Medical Dresser (CMD)

Essential wound care, dressing, and patient assistance skills.
